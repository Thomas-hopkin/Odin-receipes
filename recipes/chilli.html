Chilli Day Chilli

Back

Description

A lovely homely meal to be enjoyed on a cold Winters day. Easy to cook and super tasty!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chilli beans
  • 1 can of corn
  • 500grams of beef mince
  • 2 tbls of worscestershire sauce
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cook beef mince until brown
  2. Add Worsestershire sauce
  3. Add chopped tomatoes & corn
  4. Add stock cube
  5. Add chilli beans & corn
  6. Enjoy
