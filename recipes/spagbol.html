Description

An all time favourite meal for the family. Can't go wrong!

Ingredients

  • 1 x Pasta sauce
  • 1 x crushed tomatoes
  • Two gloves of garlic
  • 1/4 packet of spaghetti
  • 500grams beef mince
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion

Steps

  1. Finely chop garlic, onion & carrots
  2. Fry garlic and onion until fragrant
  3. Cook carrots
  4. Cook mince
  5. Pour in pasta sauce
  6. Pour in crushed tomatoes
  7. Leave to simmer for 5 minutes
  8. Cook pasta
  9. EAT!
